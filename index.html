Corruption in Kenya

Brief on corruption

Corruption is a dishonest conduct by those in power, typically involving in bribery.

Kenya's competitiveness is held back by high corruption levels that penetrates every sector of our economy.

Institutions which were design for regulation of the relationship between citizens and the state,
are being used instead for personal enrichment of public officials and other corrupt private agents

Causes of corruption in Kenya

The list is the causes of corruption in Kenya;

  1. Bad governance
  2. Political patronage
  3. Lack of political will
  4. Weak or absence of management system
  5. Lack of professional integrity

Effects of corruption in Kenya

Here are the effects of corruption in Kenya

  1. poor infrastructre
  2. increased cost of goods and services
  3. increased poverty
  4. reduced investment in our economy
  5. unemployment

Measures that have been taken to fight Corruption in Kenya

There are measures that our current government have taken to fight corruption. This include;

  1. Develop an online platform that would allow citizens and customers to report bribe demands
  2. Gorvenment to introduce travel wallet cards for all state and CEOs ofstate corporation
  3. Ordered immediate vetting of all staffs at KRA and customs and immigration department
  4. Alleviate poverty
